Chili & Pepita Mole sauce

Who doesn't love a mole sauce with their tamale?

ingredients
  • 1 c raw pepitas
  • 6 ancho chiles
  • 6 guajillo chiles
  • 1.5 c water
  • 1 tsp salt
directions
  1. toast chiles in oven @ 350 for ~3 minutes
  2. toast pepitas for ~4 minutes on a skillet
  3. blend with water & salt to taste
