¡TAMALES!

homemade tamales

tamalese are great for you, and are easy to take with yoU!

ingredients
  • tamale masa
  • water
  • salt
  • baking soda
  • butter
  • corn starch
  • dried corn husks
  1. grind ~1.5 tsp salt
  2. mix salt with 2 tsp of corn starch
  3. mix above with 1 tsp of baking soda
  4. pour 4 cups of masa into a large bowl
  5. cut 1/2 # butter into 16 chunks
  6. mix all above
  7. incorporate 3 cups of water into above
  8. separate dough into 16 portions and fill corn husks
  9. steam tamales on top of the beans (whenver you make them next) for 1 hour or more.
